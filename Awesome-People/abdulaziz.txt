Facts about the name Abdulaziz

The name Abdulaziz (or Abd al-Aziz) means "servant of the Mighty" or "servant of the Powerful".
It's a male Arabic name and surname, often abbreviated to Aziz.
The name is composed of two parts: "Abd" meaning "servant" and "Aziz" meaning "the Mighty" or "the Powerful".
In Islamic tradition, Aziz is one of the names of God.
